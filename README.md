# Avetta-Ready Annual QHSE Assurance Dashboard (Excel)

Public documentation for an annual **Quality, Health, Safety & Environment (QHSE)** dashboard designed to support **Avetta** reporting requirements and provide a company-wide, audit-ready view of QHSE performance.

> This repository intentionally contains ** screenshots only**.  
> The operational Excel workbooks and raw datasets are **not published** to protect privacy, confidentiality, and compliance requirements.

---

## Why this matters
This is not just a dashboard — it is an **assurance and compliance artifact**:
- Consolidates QHSE performance into **traceable KPIs**
- Produces **audit-ready outputs** aligned with Avetta-style reporting logic
- Highlights time trends and risk patterns to reduce incidents and improve safety outcomes
- Supports executive decisions with consistent definitions and repeatable annual updates

---

## What it covers

### 1) Core KPIs
- **Total Number of Workers (TNW)**
- **Total Work Hours (TWH)**
- **Number of Incidents**
- **Unsafe Conditions**
- **HSE Trainings**

### 2) Risk & Unsafe Conditions Analytics
- Risk categorization by type and recurrence
- Pattern detection to prioritize prevention actions

### 3) Time Intelligence (Monthly / Annual)
- Monthly trends to detect seasonality
- Year-over-year comparison to measure improvement and corrective-action impact

### 4) Internal Adoption Indicators
- Training-to-workforce ratio (safety culture signal)
- Team compliance signals via KPI movement

---

## Avetta & Audit readiness
- Designed to be **Avetta-ready** in terms of KPI logic and reporting structure (e.g., TNW/TWH and incident information).
- Includes clear definitions/methodology (Definitions) to support audits.
- Built for annual repeatability: consistent KPI definitions enable reliable comparisons across years.

---

## Dashboard outputs (visual design)
- KPI cards (TNW, TWH, Incidents)
- Bar charts (trainings per month, workforce vs hours)
- Line chart (incident trends over time)
- Risk/unsafe condition distributions
- (Optional) Risk map / hotspots depending on available data

---

## Annual refresh process
Updated **every year** by:
- Loading the new-year dataset
- Refreshing pivots/queries
- Keeping KPI definitions stable for consistent year-over-year analysis

---

## Data privacy (mandatory for public sharing)
Screenshots must not expose:
- Names of individuals, sites, internal IDs
- Any sensitive operational or commercial details
- Confidential values (use blur/redaction or ranges if needed)

- Microsoft Excel (dashboard, pivots, KPI layout)
